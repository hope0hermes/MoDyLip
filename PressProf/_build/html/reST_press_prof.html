py3_press_prof Docstrings¶

Measurement of the pressure profile across planar bilayers.

This programs does statistics on the instantaneous realizations of the pressure profile of planar bilayers. The program assumes that the bilayer normal points in the z-direction.

class py3_press_prof.PressTens(tens=[], tens_err=[])¶

Pressure tensor across the bilayer.

The components of the pressure tensor are stored in a 2D, nested list of N rows and 10 columns:

height P_{xx} P_{yy} P_{zz} P_{xy} P_{xz} P_{yz}
z_{0} %f %f %f %f %f %f
z_{1} %f %f %f %f %f %f
. . . . . . .
. . . . . . .
. . . . . . .
z_{N-1} %f %f %f %f %f %f

where {x,y,z} are Cartesian coordinates and the numerical subindex in the 1st column stands for the spatial discretization along the bilayer normal (which is assumed to point along the z-direction). A similar data structure stores the local standard deviation of each tensor component.

Parameters:
  • prof – Average value of the different pressure tensor components.
  • prof_err – Standard deviation of the pressure tensor components.
